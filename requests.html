Requests


For all applications developed by FZG Apps, we are committed to ensuring transparency and giving you control over your personal data.

Users may request the deletion of some or all of their personal data associated with our apps.


To request data deletion, please follow these steps:

1. Send an email to fzgapps@gmail.com.

2. Clearly specify the application and related data you wish to have deleted.


Upon receiving your request, we will process it promptly. Please note:

• Certain data may be retained to comply with legal or regulatory requirements or to fulfill legitimate business purposes.

• Any retained data and its retention period will be clearly communicated to you if applicable.


If you have further questions or concerns about data deletion, please do not hesitate to contact us at fzgapps@gmail.com.
